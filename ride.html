THEMED CLASS

Building the foundation for all movements and types of training in Ground Zero. Build is a full body focused hypertrophy workout interlaced with periodic spurts of interval training. Expect to feel every muscle group working whether individually or in large compound movements.

RIDE OR DIE 45/60

Our Ride or Die class is an advanced version of our signature Ride classes. Expect tougher movements, harder sets and increased intensity, all set to heart-pumping and beat dropping playlists. If you’re gunning for that extra challenge – look out for the 60-minute version of this class, perfect for riders who want to test their endurance.

First time? We got you covered.

  1. Be Prepared

    2. Hydrate well and eat a little something before class. Wear something comfortable and be ready to move and sweat. We suggest form fitting shorts or tights and a breathable top.

  2. Check In

    3. Arrive early. Do yourself a favour and come to the studio at least 20 minutes before class starts to sign in at our front desk. If you’re not signed in at least 4 minutes before class your spot may be released.

  3. Gear Up

    4. Cleated shoes are provided free of charge. We use SPD cleats so feel free to bring your own compatible shoes. We also have water available for sale in our studio.

  4. Set Up

    5. Give our instructors or front desk member a wave and we’ll help you with your bike. Let us help you get set up correctly and safely.
